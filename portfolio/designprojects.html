Design Projects

Buff Portal App

Applied mixed‑methods research and iterative testing to reimagine the CU Buff Portal as a mobile‑first experience for CU Boulder students. Created a high‑fidelity prototype focused on simplifying core tasks, clarifying navigation, and addressing usability gaps.

Resolution

Investigated user needs in wellness spaces and designed features of a mobile app to support routine‑building and connection. Designed personas, messaging flows, and a high‑fidelity match‑page prototype to support a more structured and socially engaging wellness experience.

Music club!

Designed and built a community website for a music club, providing features for members to share their music, post and discover events, and explore curated recommendations from the community.

How can we stop people from doomscrolling?

Designed and developed a conceptual website demonstrating how Instagram could promote outdoor activity by using real-time weather data to reduce doomscrolling and encourage users to engage with their environment.

Blossoming

Designed a minimalistic movie poster for a challenge, utilizing only two images and incorporating 75% negative space.
